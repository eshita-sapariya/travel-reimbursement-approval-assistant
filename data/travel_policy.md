# Travel Reimbursement Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 01 January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy defines the rules for reimbursing travel expenses incurred by employees while performing official business duties.

The objective is to ensure fair, transparent, and compliant reimbursement of business-related travel expenses.

---

# 2. Eligibility

Employees are eligible for reimbursement only when:

- Travel is officially approved.
- Travel is for business purposes.
- Expenses are supported by valid receipts wherever applicable.
- Claims are submitted within 90 days of travel completion.

Claims submitted after 90 days may be rejected.

---

# 3. Reimbursable Expense Categories

The following expenses are reimbursable:

- Flight
- Train
- Bus
- Hotel Accommodation
- Meals
- Taxi / Cab
- Local Transportation
- Internet Charges (Business Use)
- Conference Registration
- Visa Fees

Any expense outside these categories requires manual review.

---

# 4. Flight Policy

Domestic Travel

- Economy Class is reimbursable.
- Premium Economy is allowed only with prior approval.
- Business Class requires Director approval.

International Travel

- Economy Class is reimbursable.
- Business Class requires prior approval.

First Class travel is not reimbursable.

Required Documents

- Booking confirmation
- Flight invoice
- Boarding pass (if requested)

---

# 5. Hotel Accommodation

Domestic Travel

Maximum reimbursement:

₹5,000 per night

International Travel

Maximum reimbursement:

₹12,000 per night

Employees should select reasonably priced hotels.

Luxury hotels require prior approval.

Hotel receipt is mandatory.

If hotel charges exceed the allowed limit, reimbursement will be capped at the policy limit unless pre-approved.

---

# 6. Meals

Maximum reimbursement per day

Breakfast

₹300

Lunch

₹600

Dinner

₹800

Maximum total meal reimbursement per day

₹1,500

Alcoholic beverages are not reimbursable.

Entertainment expenses are not reimbursable.

Tips exceeding 10% of the bill are not reimbursable.

Meal receipts are mandatory.

---

# 7. Taxi and Local Transportation

Taxi expenses are reimbursable only for business-related travel.

Maximum reimbursement:

₹2,000 per day

Ride receipts are mandatory.

Personal sightseeing and leisure travel are not reimbursable.

---

# 8. Internet Charges

Business internet usage is reimbursable.

Maximum:

₹500 per day

Personal internet usage is not reimbursable.

---

# 9. Conference Registration

Conference fees are reimbursable if:

- Approved before travel
- Related to business objectives

Conference invoice must be attached.

---

# 10. Visa Fees

Business visa charges are reimbursable.

Supporting payment receipt is mandatory.

---

# 11. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will not be reimbursed:

- Alcohol
- Personal shopping
- Entertainment
- Tourist activities
- Family member expenses
- Traffic fines
- Parking fines
- Personal hotel upgrades
- First Class flight tickets
- Luxury hotel upgrades

Claims containing only non-reimbursable expenses will be rejected.

---

# 12. Required Documents

Every reimbursement claim should contain:

- Travel Type
- Expense Category
- Expense Amount
- Expense Date
- Vendor Name
- Business Purpose
- Receipt (where applicable)

Claims missing mandatory information may be routed for manual review.

---

# 13. Approval Matrix

| Claim Amount | Approval Required |
|--------------|------------------|
| ₹0 – ₹10,000 | Reporting Manager |
| ₹10,001 – ₹50,000 | Department Head |
| ₹50,001 – ₹100,000 | Finance Director |
| Above ₹100,000 | CFO |

---

# 14. Reimbursement Rules

The following business rules apply:

- Expenses exceeding policy limits are reimbursed only up to the permitted amount.
- Excess amounts are deducted.
- Missing receipts may result in manual review.
- Duplicate receipts are not allowed.
- Future travel dates are invalid.
- Claims older than 90 days may be rejected.
- Personal expenses are not reimbursable.

---

End of Travel Reimbursement Policy
